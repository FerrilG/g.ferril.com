EXPERIENCE DESIGN

Design with with your users in mind to evolve the experience of your application. I focus on thoughtful evaluation of your users tasks to understand who they are. This task based approach offers a holistic view of your application and identifies areas for improvement.

INTERACTION DESIGN

UI design requires an understanding of technical limitations, web design, and supportive framework knowledge. My skills assist both designers and developers through the creation of custom design systems (both static and interactive), interactive prototypes, responsive layouts, detailed annotated wireframes, and much more.

INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE

Adding content is fairly easy; adding cohesive and intuitive content at large scale is not. Communicating with users and stakeholers can be a challenge. With the assistance of sitemaps and user flows I construct diagrams that ensure newly added items align seamlessly and are methodical with user's in mind based on their goals.
